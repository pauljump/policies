PaperClaw Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

PaperClaw is a paper trading app for iMessage. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information. PaperClaw does not require an account, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

The app uses anonymous, opaque participant identifiers provided by Apple's iMessage framework. These identifiers are unique per conversation and cannot be used to identify you across different group chats or outside of the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your portfolio data (cash balance, holdings, and transaction history) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to send a trade message in iMessage.

Data Sent to Our Server

When you place a trade, a summary (ticker, direction, amount) may be sent to our server for leaderboard tracking. This data is associated only with your anonymous participant ID, not your real identity.

Third-Party Services

PaperClaw fetches market data from:

  • Yahoo Finance — stock and options pricing
  • Polymarket — prediction market data

These requests contain only the ticker symbol or market query. No personal data is sent to these services.

Children's Privacy

PaperClaw does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app involves simulated trading with no real money.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
