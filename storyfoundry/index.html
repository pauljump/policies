StoryFoundry Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

StoryFoundry is a social stories app that helps families with autistic children prepare for real-world situations. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We use anonymous authentication. StoryFoundry does not require an email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information to use. You are signed in anonymously via Firebase Authentication.

We collect the following anonymous, non-identifying data:

  • A random device identifier (UUID) generated locally on your device
  • An anonymous Firebase user ID
  • Story creation events (title, panel count) for analytics
  • Outcome ratings you choose to record (great, ok, tough)

Data Stored on Your Device

Your stories, child profile context, and outcome history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data stays on your device unless you choose to share a story with the community.

Community Sharing

If you choose to share a story with the StoryFoundry community:

  • Personal photos from your camera roll are automatically removed before sharing
  • Only place photos from Google Maps and app-generated illustrations are included
  • Your story is associated with your anonymous user ID, not your real identity
  • Other users can view, import, and rate shared stories
  • Shared stories remain available to the community even if you delete them from your personal library

Third-Party Services

StoryFoundry uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI — to generate story text and illustrations. Your child's name, selected place, and concerns are sent to OpenAI's API to create personalized stories. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data processed by their API.
  • Google Places — to search for places and retrieve place photos. Only search queries and place IDs are sent.
  • Firebase (Google) — for anonymous authentication, community story storage, and analytics events. No personally identifiable information is stored.

Children's Privacy

StoryFoundry is designed for parents and caregivers of children. The app does not collect data directly from children. All interactions are intended to be parent-directed. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Local data can be deleted at any time by removing the app. Community-shared stories can be deleted from the community by the user who shared them. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form and cannot be traced to individual users.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
